Background

My senior year, I was the editor-in-chief of my high school newspaper, The Campanile. I joined the paper in my sophomore year and designed over 18 page spreads using Adobe InDesign and Photoshop. The graphic design skills I built have served me well in my college career when creating visually compelling graphics and presentations.

Awards

  • 3rd place Single-Page Layout, The Campanile, “Common Ground: Respecting Political Ideologies in the Classroom”; CFPW (California Federation Press Women) Journalism award
  • 3rd place Graphics, The Campanile, “The Voices Behind the Votes”; CFPW Journalism award
  • Honorable Mention Double Truck Design, The Campanile Palo Alto High School, “The Voices Behind the Votes”; CFPW Journalism award
