Background

My senior year, I was the editor-in-chief of my high school newspaper, The Campanile. I joined the paper in my sophomore year and wrote 15 articles and 8 editorials. The professional conversational and writing skills I learned from being a staff writer and editor-in-chief have equipped me to be a strong communicator when it comes to writing and speaking in engineering.
